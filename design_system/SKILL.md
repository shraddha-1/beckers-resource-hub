## Quick orientation
- `README.md` — full content, voice, visual, and iconography rules.
- `colors_and_type.css` — all CSS vars + class presets. Import this first.
- `assets/logos/` — Becker's Hospital Review wordmark.
- `assets/icons/` — house SVG icons (events, webinars, whitepapers, podcasts).
- `preview/` — design-system-tab preview cards (browse to see palette, type, components at a glance).
- `ui_kits/bhr-web/` — website UI kit (homepage, article view, cards, nav, footer).
- `ui_kits/newsletter/` — daily email kit.

## The 30-second brand brief
- **Tagline triad:** Professional · Trusted · Engaging.
- **Colors:** deep navy `#091F3E` (primary), crimson `#9A1B2A` (accent — wordmark, kickers, links, CTAs). Cool-tinted whites.
- **Type:** Noto Serif for headlines (sentence case), Fira Sans for body/UI.
- **Voice:** third-person, reporter-style, number-dense. No emoji. No hype.
- **Visuals:** editorial, square-cornered or 2–4px radii, minimal shadows, no gradients/illustrations, real healthcare photography.
